    TRAIN THE TRAINER FOR SCHOOLS

    Provision will be made for the school to provide their name, address, phone number of Proprietor/Key contact person, email ID, and school website where available.

    Terms and Conditions for Abacus Mental Math Train the Trainer Program for Schools

    1. Duration

    This agreement remains in effect until one complete cycle of the five levels of the program has been completed.

    2. Program Structure

    • Program Delivery: The Company will provide the School with the Abacus Mental Arithmetic program, structured into five levels.
    • Facility Use: The School will make its facilities available to effectively run the program.
    • Parental Engagement: The School will notify and encourage all her parents to enroll their children in the program through newsletters or other appropriate channels.
    • Student Enrollment: The School will ensure that students in Grades [Specify Grades] register for the program.
    • Lesson Schedule: The program will be taught twice a week for primary students and once a week for secondary students.

    3. Materials

    The materials required for the program and their prices are as follows:

    • Teachers Abacus: 60,000.00
    • Students Abacus: 5,000.00
    • Books: 2,000.00
    • Price Changes: Any prospective school interested in our school Train the Trainer program should contact us first to know if the prices stated on our website are still valid before making payments. ABM will inform registered schools of any price changes in advance.
    • Resale Option: The School may sell these materials to students at a slightly higher price to cover administrative costs.
    • Source of Materials: All instructional materials must be sourced from the Company.
    • Copyright Compliance: The School is prohibited from photocopying any program materials as it violates copyright laws.
    • Payment for Materials: Payment for materials must be made before delivery to maintain sufficient stock levels.
    • Order Timing: The School must place orders for materials at least two weeks before they are needed.
    • Waybill Costs: The School will bear the cost of shipping for materials ordered.

    Teacher Training

    • Teacher Information: The School will provide the names, phone numbers, and email IDs of teachers to be trained.
    • Training Fee: The School will pay 7,000.00 per teacher, covering the cost of materials for Level 1 training.
    • Subsequent Training: For future levels, the School will only pay for the new book required.
    • Authorized Trainers: Training will be conducted by the Companys certified master trainer.
    • Training Duration: The training will last four days.
    • Certified Teachers: Only teachers trained and certified by the Company are permitted to teach the program.
    • Supervisory Visits: The Company will regularly send staff to the School to ensure compliance with operational procedures.
    • Training Location: Training will be conducted at the Companys office. If necessary, training may be held at an alternative venue by mutual agreement.
    • Online Training Option: The School may opt for online training, where it would make payment for her teachers subject to the terms and conditions for online teacher training.
    • Onsite Training Costs: If training occurs at the Schools premises, the School will pay the trainer an honorarium of 150,000.00 to cover the trainers transport and lunch. This amount is negotiable.
    • Onsite Trainings Outside Imo State: The school shall provide two-way flight tickets, airport pick-up, hotel accommodation, and meals during the training, in addition to the honorarium stated.

    5. Payment Terms

    All payments for the program should be made to the Companys account:

    Bank: United Bank for Africa (UBA) Plc
    Account Number: 1019303849
    Payments: are to be made in full.

    Payment Method: Payments should be made via direct deposit or electronic transfer. The Company does not accept cash payments in hand.

    6. Class Recording

    The trainer may record video clips of the class for business promotion purposes. By signing up for the class, the client consents to the use of these clips.

    Online Abacus Mental Math Teachers Training

    Provision will be made for the trainee to supply their name, address, email ID, and phone number.

    Terms and Conditions for Online Abacus Mental Math Teacher Training

    1. Payment Terms

    Payment must be made in full before course materials are provided and a trainer is assigned. Payments should be made via electronic transfer to our account:

    Account Name: Abacus Brain Maths Nig. Ltd
    Bank: UBA Plc
    Account Number: 1019303849
    Training Fee: 50,000.00

    Prospective trainees should contact us to be sure that the prices quoted on our website are still valid before making payments.

    2. Training Schedule

    The training program lasts for four days, with sessions starting at 8:00 PM daily. Trainees are expected to be online at least five minutes before the scheduled start time. If a trainee is not present and the trainer waits for 30 minutes, the session will be considered as completed.

    3. Rescheduling Policy

    If a trainee cannot attend a session, they must inform the trainer at least three hours in advance to reschedule. If the trainee misses the rescheduled session, no further rescheduling will be allowed.

    4. Technical Requirements

    Trainees must have a functional laptop or a good smartphone and a strong internet connection to participate in the training.

    5. Internet Connectivity

    The trainer is not responsible for any disruptions caused by poor internet connectivity on the trainees end. If connectivity issues prevent a trainee from fully participating, the trainer will provide links to prerecorded classes to cover the missed session(s).

    6. Class Recording

    The trainer may record clips of the online class for business promotion purposes. By signing up for the class, the client consents to the use of these clips.

    7. Agreement to Terms

    By enrolling in this training, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to these terms and conditions.

    Kids Online Class

    Provision will be made for the parents to provide the name of their child to be registered, age, email ID, and address.

    Terms and Conditions for Online Abacus Mental Math Classes

    1. Commencement of Training and Classes

    Training or classes will commence only after full payment of the training or class fee has been received.

    2. Provision of Email ID

    Parents are required to provide a valid email ID, preferably Gmail, for receiving links to the class sessions and workbooks for their wards.

    3. Training and Class Platform

    The training or classes will be conducted using Google Meet.

    4. Class Schedule and Fees

    There are two options for scheduling:

    • Twice a Week: Training sessions or classes will be held two times a week for a monthly fee of 50,000.00 (Fifty Thousand Naira).
    • Once a Week: Training sessions or classes will be held once a week for a monthly fee of 30,000.00 (Thirty Thousand Naira).

    Each session will last between 45 minutes to 1 hour.

    5. Punctuality

    If the child is late, the teacher will wait for 30 minutes from the scheduled start time. After this period, the teacher may leave, and the class will be considered held.

    6. Cancellation Policy

    Parents must notify the teacher at least 3 hours in advance if they cannot attend a class. Failure to do so will result in forfeiting the lesson, with no make-up class provided.

    If a class is canceled due to the teacher's fault, a make-up class will be arranged, and the client or trainee will be informed of the new date and time.

    7. Technical Requirements

    Parents must ensure they provide their wards with a functional phone, laptop, or tablet with a strong and stable internet connection to participate in the training or classes.

    8. Liability for Internet Issues

    The teacher will not be held responsible for any disruptions to the training or classes caused by poor internet connectivity, whether it's from their end or from that of the student.

    9. Mitigation for Poor Connectivity

    If a trainee's or client's participation is hindered due to poor internet connectivity, the trainer or teacher will provide links to pre-recorded classes to cover the missed session(s).

    10. Class Recording

    The teacher may record clips of the online class for business promotion purposes. By signing up for the training or class, the trainee or client consents to the use of these clips.

    By enrolling in this training or class, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to these terms and conditions.
